# US Business Lead Research & Data Collection

## Project Overview
I performed comprehensive web research for a client who needed high-quality business leads in the US home services sector. The client provided specific niches, and I researched, verified, and organized company information into clean, structured Excel spreadsheets.

**Key Niches Researched:**
- HVAC
- Roofing
- Window Installation
- Flooring
- Kitchen Remodeling
- Gardening / Landscaping

## What I Delivered
  - Collected accurate company data including:
  - Business Name
  - Phone Number (verified)
  - Physical Location (City & State)
  - Google Maps Rating & Reviews Count
  - Website (where available)
  - Additional relevant details

- Maintained high data accuracy through manual verification
- Organized everything in well-structured Excel format for easy use

## Tools & Skills Demonstrated
- Advanced Web Research & Data Collection
- Data Cleaning and Verification
- Microsoft Excel (formulas, filters, organization)
- Attention to Detail & Accuracy
- Client Requirement Analysis

## Results
Successfully delivered multiple batches of qualified leads that the client could directly use for outreach. Reduced invalid contacts significantly through verification process.
